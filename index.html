MAES UTSA

MAES UTSA asked for a website that featured information about their organization, kept their members up to date with events and news, and made it easy for new members to join. I designed a website from scratch for them that met all their needs and more. I used bootstrap since most of the users access the website via mobile devices while also giving the website a nice and simple look. I implemented the Google Calendar under events so members are able to easily check upcoming events. I also created a "Join" button that links new members to the official MAES website, so they can easily join. Extra features that I included was a officers section with a picture of every officer and created a "Donate" button that links to the organization's PayPal account.
